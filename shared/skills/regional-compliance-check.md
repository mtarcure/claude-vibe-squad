# Regional Compliance Check

Surface locale legal/cultural constraints on content — flag risk, do not adjudicate.

## Steps
1. Identify the target locale's constraints: claims/advertising law, age/rating, disclosure requirements, restricted content.
2. Check the content against each; flag every risk with the specific rule it touches.
3. Raise the task's risk level upward when a regulatory constraint applies.
4. Surface findings to the operator/counsel — do NOT make a legal determination.
5. Require independent native review for high-stakes or regulated locales; state explicit status when unavailable.

## Acceptance
- Each risk is flagged with the specific constraint it implicates.
- No legal adjudication is made in-role; findings are surfaced for human/counsel.
- Regulated/high-stakes locales are flagged for native review and the task risk is raised.
