# Citation Audit

Audit every citation in a piece of content: resolve it, read it, and confirm it supports the claim.

## Steps
1. Extract every citation/link/reference from the content.
2. Resolve each one and record the accessed-at time; a citation that 404s or was invented is unverifiable.
3. Read the source and confirm it actually SUPPORTS the claim — not merely mentions the topic; preserve quote context.
4. Check publication/event/access dates and any retractions or corrections.
5. Label vendor/provider performance claims as vendor claims unless reproduced on a Vibe-Squad benchmark.
6. Report counts: citations checked / resolved / unverified.

## Acceptance
- Every citation is resolved (with accessed-at) or marked unverifiable.
- The supports-check is done per claim; quotes keep their context.
- Vendor claims are labeled; no corroboration is fabricated.
