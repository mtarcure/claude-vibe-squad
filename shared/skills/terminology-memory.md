# Terminology Memory

Maintain glossary consistency and do-not-translate discipline across localization jobs.

## Steps
1. Build or load the locale glossary and the do-not-translate list (brand names, product terms, code).
2. Apply pinned terms consistently across the whole job.
3. Record any new term with its rationale and per-locale rendering back to the glossary (chrono-vault).
4. Resolve conflicts against the authoritative glossary entry, not ad hoc.

## Acceptance
- Glossary terms are applied consistently; do-not-translate terms are never translated.
- New terms are recorded with rationale and per-locale rendering.
- Term conflicts resolve to the authoritative entry.
